Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small meetup for tech enthusiasts. Over the years, it has evolved into a global platform for innovation and collaboration.

Our Mission

TechCon strives to inspire, educate, and connect individuals in the tech industry through engaging talks, workshops, and networking opportunities.

Notable Past Speakers

Ada Lovelace: Recognized as the first computer programmer, Ada inspired generations of women in tech.

Alan Turing: His work laid the foundation for modern computing and artificial intelligence.
